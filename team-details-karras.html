Alex Karras

Faculty Assistant to Lynn Kaack, Slava Jankin and Lion Hirth

Alex Karras works at Hertie as the Faculty Assistant to Lynn Kaack, Slava Jankin and Lion Hirth. He is a 2017 graduate of Humboldt University of Berlin with a Bachelor’s degree in Regional Studies Asia/Africa with a focus on Japanese language and culture. In 2014/15, he studied at Chūō University in Tokyo for one year. He is trained as a management assistant for audiovisual media.
