Our vision - To make Essex national leaders in using the power of data science and AI to tackle public policy challenges.

Our roadmap to accelerate us towards realising our bold ambition:
 * Make Essex a place that is exemplar for integration of data across public bodies
 * Deliver predictive analytics based on ethically high standards
 * Build a sustainable data infrastructure
 * Create the best data science / analytical capabilities in the UK to benefit our people and communities

Partnership diagnostic and capability assessment

We'll work across the whole Essex to understand where we are now in relation to our analytics maturity journey and identify our existing capabilities and infrastructure across the partnership. This includes mapping out all existing work to improve levels of awareness and identify what impact we have made and where to next.

Develop joint partnership skills & capabilities

Partners will develop joined up skills and capabilities across the whole system to implement data-driven solutions towards prevention and benefit local people and businesses.

Create dynamic learning opportunities for social good

There is a need to have a continuous view on what best in class / cutting edge looks like, ensuring we are constantly learning from others and developing our talent locally to the benefit citizens and local businesses of Essex.

Developing an Essex standard

Building a set of guiding principles that all partners can sign up to, will ensure that we are following the highest possible ethical and protection standards in projects using data science and AI.

Project Sponsors

Essex system sponsors of this work include Gavin Jones Chief Executive of Essex County Council, Stephen Kavanagh, Chief Constable of Essex Police and Anthony Forster Chancellor of the University of Essex.
