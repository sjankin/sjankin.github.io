About me

At the intersection of artificial intelligence and political science, my research develops and applies advanced computational methods to address complex societal challenges. I focus on creating innovative AI techniques, using these to gain insights into political dynamics, and applying this knowledge to critical areas such as climate change and sustainable development.

Research Focus

My work integrates three interconnected areas:

  1. AI and Computational Methods: I develop cutting-edge computational techniques, focusing on:
    • Creating LLM-based multi-agent systems for complex simulations
    • Advancing machine learning and natural language processing for political analysis
    • Innovating in large-scale data analysis and multi-agent modelling
  2. Political Dynamics and Governance: Using these computational tools, I investigate political processes and decision-making:
    • Simulating various political environments, from committee negotiations to policy-making processes
    • Exploring how AI can enhance democratic processes and informed decision-making
    • Analysing complex political systems and their responses to societal challenges
    • Understanding the cognitive aspects of political persuasion
  3. Applications in Climate Change, Health, and Sustainable Development: I apply these insights to real-world challenges, particularly through my work with the Lancet Countdown, a major international collaboration tracking progress on health and climate change:
    • Developing innovative indicators for understanding climate-health governance
    • Using advanced NLP to monitor public perceptions of climate change impacts
    • Informing sustainable development strategies through data-driven insights
    • Translating complex political and environmental data into actionable policy recommendations


Impact and Future Directions

This integrated approach allows me to contribute to more resilient and effective governance systems in the face of global challenges. By progressing from methodological innovations to political analysis and then to specific policy applications, my work demonstrates how AI and computational methods can provide novel insights into political dynamics while directly informing policy in critical areas such as climate change and sustainable development.

As AI continues to evolve, so too will the questions we ask and the methods we use. I'm committed to staying at the forefront of this field, continuously exploring new ways to apply computational social science to the most pressing issues facing our societies.
